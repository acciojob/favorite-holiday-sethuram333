Paris


Paris is the capital city of France and is famous for its romantic atmosphere, historic monuments, art museums, and delicious cuisine.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Eiffel Tower – The most iconic landmark of Paris.
  2. Louvre Museum – Home to the famous Mona Lisa.
  3. Notre-Dame Cathedral – A beautiful Gothic church.
  4. Champs-Élysées – A famous shopping street.
  5. Montmartre – Known for its artistic culture.

Must-Try Foods

  • Croissant – A buttery French pastry.
  • Macarons – Sweet and colorful desserts.
  • Crêpes – Thin pancakes with sweet fillings.
  • Ratatouille – A traditional vegetable dish.
  • French Cheese – Known for its rich taste.
