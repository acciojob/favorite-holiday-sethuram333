Paris


Paris, the capital city of France, is known as the "City of Love" and is famous for its rich history, beautiful architecture, world-class museums, and delicious cuisine. It attracts millions of tourists every year who come to experience its romantic atmosphere and cultural charm.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Eiffel Tower – The most iconic landmark of Paris.
  2. Louvre Museum – Home to the famous Mona Lisa painting.
  3. Notre-Dame Cathedral – A masterpiece of Gothic architecture.
  4. Champs-Élysées – The most beautiful avenue in the world.
  5. Montmartre – A charming hilltop area known for its artistic vibe.

Must-Try Foods

  • Croissants – A buttery and flaky pastry.
  • Macarons – Colorful and sweet delights.
  • Crêpes – Thin pancakes filled with chocolate or fruits.
  • Ratatouille – A traditional vegetable dish.
  • French Cheese – Famous for its rich flavors.
